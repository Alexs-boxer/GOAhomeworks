Ford has officially announced that the 2017 Ford Fusion sedan in the Energi plug-in version can travel 610 miles (981.6 km) on a single tank of gas with a fully charged battery 60 miles (96.5 km) more than before.


The 2025 Ford Fusion is estimated to have a starting price around $29,000, with higher trims potentially reaching $40,000 or more. A YouTube video states this price range is based on industry estimates, as Ford has not yet officially announced pricing. The price will vary based on trim level, engine options (including hybrid), and available features.
